Description
Establishes a system for the transliteration and/or transcription of Greek characters into Latin characters. This system applies to the characters of the Greek script, independent of the period in which it is or was used, i.e. scripts from all periods of Classic or Modern Greek. It therefore allows for polytonic Greek, though it only acknowlegdges rough breathing, and ignores all other diacritics.
